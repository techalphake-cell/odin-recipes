Meatballs

Description

A beautiful plate of meatballs, served with a delicious tomato sauce and a side of spaghetti.

Enjoy this meal as breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, and finely chopped onion. Mix until well combined.
  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs,
  4. Put them into the oven for 10 minutes one at a time
