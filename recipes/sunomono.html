Sunomono Cucumber Salad

Description

Sunomono is a delicious and simple Japanese cucumber salad made with thinly sliced cucumbers in a sweet and sour vinegar dressing. It makes a refreshing salad or light side dish with salmon

ingredients

    2 large cucumbers, peeled
  • ⅓ cup rice vinegar
  • 4 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced fresh ginger root or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste

steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cut cucumbers in half lengthwise and scoop out any large seeds. Slice crosswise into very thin slices.
  3. Mix the vinegar, sugar, ginger, and salt in a small bowl.
  4. Place the sliced cucumbers in a large bowl and pour the dressing over them.
  5. Toss to combine and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
